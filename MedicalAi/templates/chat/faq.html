🧾 Using Clinexa

After receiving an initial diagnosis, Clinexa offers a structured follow-up system to provide you with comprehensive information. Here's how it works:

  1. Initial Diagnosis: Clinexa provides a preliminary assessment of your symptoms
  2. Follow-up Options: You'll see three interactive options:
    • 💊 Recommended medication
    • 🏃 Lifestyle recommendations
    • 👨‍⚕️ When to see a doctor
  3. Interactive Flow: Click any option to see detailed information, or type "yes" to view all sections sequentially
  4. Restart Option: After viewing all sections, you can start a new symptom check

You can describe your symptoms in natural language, just like you would to a doctor. Here are some examples of what works best:

  • Specific symptoms (e.g., "I have a headache and fever" or "My stomach hurts after eating")
  • Duration of symptoms (e.g., "for 3 days" or "since yesterday")
  • Severity (e.g., "mild pain" or "severe headache")
  • Any factors that make it better or worse

For best results, be as specific as possible about your symptoms and when they started.

🔍 Understanding Results

Clinexa provides information in several structured sections to help you understand your condition and next steps:

  • 💊 Medication: Information about potential medications that might be relevant, including over-the-counter and prescription options
  • 🏃 Lifestyle Recommendations: Suggestions for lifestyle changes that may help manage your symptoms
  • 👨‍⚕️ When to See a Doctor: Guidance on when professional medical attention is recommended
  • 🔍 Additional Notes: Any other relevant information or considerations about your condition

You can access these sections individually or view them all by typing "yes" after your diagnosis.

Clinexa asks for more details when it needs additional information to provide an accurate assessment. This typically happens when:

  • The initial symptoms could indicate multiple conditions
  • The description lacks specific details about duration, severity, or location
  • Key symptoms that would help narrow down possibilities are missing
  • The AI needs to distinguish between similar conditions

Providing more details helps Clinexa give you a more accurate and helpful response.

If you believe the result is incorrect, you can try rephrasing your symptoms or providing more details. However, always consult with a healthcare professional for medical advice.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

If you're not seeing the follow-up options after a diagnosis, try these steps:

  1. Ensure you've received an initial diagnosis - follow-up options appear after the first response
  2. Check your internet connection - a weak connection might prevent options from loading
  3. Try refreshing the page - sometimes a simple refresh can resolve display issues
  4. Type "yes" in the chat - this should display all available follow-up information
  5. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser

If you're still experiencing issues, please contact our support team for assistance.
